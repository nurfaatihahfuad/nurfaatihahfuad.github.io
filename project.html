Design thinking project

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Design thinking serves as a powerful framework for tackling complex challenges in the realm of Big Data and Artificial Intelligence innovation. This collaborative approach empowers students to develop cutting-edge solutions through a structured yet creative process. By following the five key stages - empathy, define, ideate, prototype, and testing - students gain invaluable experience in understanding user needs, identifying core problems, generating innovative ideas, creating working prototypes, and refining solutions through testing. In today's rapidly evolving tech landscape, this methodology is particularly crucial as it cultivates essential workforce skills such as teamwork, critical problem-solving, effective communication, analytical thinking, creativity, and leadership. Students are required to meticulously document their journey through each stage, creating a comprehensive record of their thought process and development. This systematic approach not only enhances their learning experience but also prepares them for real-world challenges in the dynamic fields of Big Data and AI, where innovation and adaptability are paramount.
