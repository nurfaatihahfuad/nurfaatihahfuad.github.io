ULRS1032 :
KURSUS INTEGRITI DAN ANTI-RASUAH

This course covers the basic concepts of corruption including integrity values, anti-corruption, forms of corrupt behavior, abuse of power in daily activities and organizations, as well as methods of corruption prevention. Corruption-related cases are also discussed. Teaching and learning methods are implemented in the form of 'experiential learning' through individual and group activities. At the end of this course, students will be able to understand integrity practices, concepts of corruption, anti-corruption and abuse of power, as well as corruption prevention in society and organizations.

Project

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Case Study: School Principal's Corruption Case A school principal was sentenced to eight months imprisonment under Section 165 of the Penal Code after being found guilty of two corruption charges. The principal received a bribe of RM 4,400.00 from a canteen operator in 2014 to extend the school canteen's contract. This case raises several discussion points regarding the factors that led to this corruption, the appropriateness of the punishment imposed, and the effectiveness of such sentences in raising awareness among society. The case serves as a stark reminder of corruption in educational institutions and highlights how positions of authority can be misused for personal gain. The relatively modest sum involved and the severe consequences faced by the principal demonstrate the zero-tolerance approach towards corruption in public service, regardless of the amount involved. The discussion questions examine: 1. The underlying factors that led to this incident 2. The appropriateness of the punishment imposed 3. The effectiveness of such sentences in deterring corruption and raising public awareness
